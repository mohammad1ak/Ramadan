What Is Ramadan

Ramadan is the ninth month of the Islamic lunar calendar, observed by Muslims worldwide as a sacred period of fasting, prayer, reflection, and community.

What We Do In Ramadan

During Ramadan, Muslims abstain from food, drink, smoking, and other physical needs from dawn until sunset.
